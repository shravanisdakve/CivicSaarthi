# CivicSaarthi — LinkedIn Launch Draft

## 🚀 Empowering 900M+ Voters with Gemini AI

I'm excited to share **CivicSaarthi**, a privacy-first, official-source-guided election readiness companion built for the Google PromptWars Challenge 2!

Election processes can be complex and overwhelming. CivicSaarthi turns that complexity into a 9-step guided journey, helping citizens move from "just curious" to "voter ready" in minutes.

### ✨ Key Features:
- **Guided Election Journey**: A step-by-step interactive walkthrough of the entire process.
- **CivicSaarthi AI**: A neutral, Gemini-powered assistant grounded in official ECI knowledge.
- **Privacy-First Design**: No Aadhaar, no VoterID, no PII. Just pure civic guidance.
- **Offline/PWA Support**: Works even on low connectivity with a local knowledge fallback.
- **Multilingual**: Native support for English, Hindi, and Marathi.
- **Google Maps Integration**: Easily find nearby Election Offices and Help Centers.

### 🛠️ Built with Google Cloud:
- **Cloud Run**: High-performance container hosting.
- **Gemini 1.5 Flash**: The brains behind our neutral civic assistant.
- **Secret Manager**: Enterprise-grade API key protection.
- **Cloud Build & Artifact Registry**: Modern CI/CD pipeline.
- **Google Maps Platform**: Helping citizens find their local help centers.
